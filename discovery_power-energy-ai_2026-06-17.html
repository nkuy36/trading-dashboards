Electricity is the hard ceiling on the AI buildout. Grid interconnect queues run years behind hyperscaler capex, pushing power behind-the-meter — gas turbines, nuclear restarts/uprates, SMRs, and fuel cells become the primary path to energized capacity. The Constellation/Vistra/Talen PPAs confirmed the generation-side shift; the fuel side collides with constrained uranium (post-Kazatomprom) and an acute HALEU shortage for the SMR fleet. Two layers of one bottleneck: who generates the megawatt, and who fuels the reactor that makes it. SMR-developer names trade on milestone/regulatory risk — size closer to T11 quantum rules than the T1–T10 mechanism. Excludes Canadian oil/gas per active preference.
